About the author

I am currently working as a senior researcher at webLyzard technology and as a researcher and external lecturer at the Department of New Media Technology at MODUL University Vienna, working on my PhD in the field of information visualisation.

I started ThreadVis in 2005 as part of my Master's Thesis titled ThreadVis for Thunderbird: A Thread Visualisation Extension for the Mozilla Thunderbird Email Client at the Institute for Information Systems and Computer Media (now Institute for Interactive Systems and Data Science), Graz University of Technology, supervised by Keith Andrews.

If you have any questions, bug reports or feature suggestions, don't hesitate to contact me.
