About the author

I am currently working as a researcher and lecturer at the Department of New Media Technology at MODUL University Vienna, working on my PhD in the field of information visualisation.

I started ThreadVis in 2005 as part of my Master's Thesis titled ThreadVis for Thunderbird: A Thread Visualisation Extension for the Mozilla Thunderbird Email Client at the Institute for Information Systems and Computer Media, Graz University of Technology, supervised by Keith Andrews.

If you have any questions, bug reports or feature suggestions, don't hesitate to contact me.
